# Bruce Wayne — Strategy Hub

This folder is Bruce Wayne's domain — the multi-year career strategy, quarterly theses, narrative architecture, and target-list management. (Bruce Wayne is one agent within the **Batman Strategic Layer**; this hub is his strategy domain, not the layer itself.)

Private working context (personal contacts, employer-specific material) stays outside this public repo. Bruce Wayne thinks about the 24-month arc to a frontier-lab AI PM role. Alfred thinks about whether the Tuesday meeting prep is done.

## What lives here

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `thesis-q3-2026.md` | Active quarterly thesis — three pillars, signposts at 30/60/90, invalidation criterion |
| `master-narrative.md` *(coming soon)* | The one-paragraph career story; surface-specific compressions for resume / LinkedIn / essay bio / interview opener |
| `target-list.md` *(coming soon)* | Frontier-lab and large-tech target ranking with current intel; refreshed each quarter |
| `monthly-retros/` *(coming soon)* | What compounded, what stalled, what to kill, what to double down on |

## Operating principles

1. **Patience.** Multi-year compounding. The 24-month arc beats the 24-hour reaction.
2. **Theatre as force multiplier.** Public artifacts are designed for impact. Considered, not gaudy.
3. **Contingency-first.** Every plan ships with B, C, D. Failure modes named before solutions.
4. **Lean cave.** What doesn't compound, ship out. Old strategies retire when superseded.

## How this folder gets updated

- **Quarterly:** new thesis file (`thesis-qN-YYYY.md`); previous quarter retired to `_archive/`
- **Monthly:** retro doc filed
- **Ad-hoc:** narrative or target-list updates whenever Oracle's intel materially shifts
- **Day 30 / Day 60 / Day 90 of each quarter:** signpost reviews; invalidation check at Day 60
